FEATURED EVENTS

Six electrifying competitions — race, tackle, duel, think and conquer. Only the boldest bots prevail.

COMPETITION
ROBO RACE
Build the fastest autonomous bot and blaze through a challenging track layout. Speed, precision and smart navigation win the crown.

ROBO SOCCER
Engineer your bot to dribble, dodge and score goals. Team-based robot soccer with real-time strategy and control challenges.
Prize AwaitsMar 23–25

ROBO KABADDI
A uniquely Indian twist — robots tag, evade and return in a digital kabaddi arena. Agility and strategy are your greatest weapons.
Prize AwaitsMar 23–25

Two bots enter, one remains. Force your opponent out of the ring in this raw test of power, weight distribution and control.

Mud, ramps, obstacles and uneven ground — design a bot tough enough to conquer every surface in this ultimate endurance race.

LOGIC ROBOTS
